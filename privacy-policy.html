Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2024

1. Data Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you book a tour, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us for support. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process and confirm your tour bookings.
  • Send you travel itineraries and updates.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve our website and services based on your feedback.

3. Cookies Usage

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand how you interact with our site, remember your preferences, and show you relevant content.

4. Payment & Security

Your payment security is our priority. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways (like Stripe or PayPal) to process transactions. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

5. User Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties without your consent.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. If you have any questions about your privacy, please contact us at info@Govolo.in.
